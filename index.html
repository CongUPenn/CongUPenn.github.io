Section 2, Final project |

PAC O'CLOCK

This design reimagines the conventional clock through a browser-based design using p5.js. In this dynamic experience, Pac-Man devours seconds, minutes, and hours, fading the original colors of the clock's beans. With this approach I hope time keeping could be more playful and provocate other thoughts that challenges traditional notions and offers a whimsical twist to checking the time.
