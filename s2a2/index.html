STEP 1 - Prepare Crust

Combine crust ingredients and press into the bottom of springform pan. Bake then cool
STEP 2 - Make Filling

Mix the cream cheese, sugar, flour, and salt. Mix in the sour cream, lemon juice, lemon zest and vanilla.
STEP 3 - Fold in Berries

Toss berries in remaining teaspoon of flour and fold into batter. Pour the batter over the cooled crust
STEP 4 - Make Topping

Stir blueberries, vanilla, sugar and lemon juice together and bring to boil/ then reduce to a simmer over medium heat. Simmer for 7 minutes until blueberries start to break down.
