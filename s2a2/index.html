make the topping
Place 1 cup of blueberries, vanilla, sugar and lemon juice in a small saucepan. Stir them together and bring to boil/ then reduce to a simmer over medium heat. Simmer for 7 minutes until blueberries start to break down. Mix cornstarch and water together and stir mixture into the blueberries, the sauce should thicken. Remove from heat and stir in the remaining blueberries. Sauce should be syrupy - remove from stove and cool. Will thicken as it cools. Once cool, stir. Adjust thickness Â½ teaspoon of water at a time to make it the perfect consistency to drizzle or spoon onto cheesecake (extra sauce can be saved and used to dollop on individual pieces as cheesecake is served).
